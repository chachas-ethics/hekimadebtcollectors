RESOURCES & FAQs

We believe in transparency. Here are some resources and common questions to help clients and debtors understand the debt collection process, rights, and available solutions.

Debt Collection Process for Clients & Debtors

For clients, our process begins with account review and contacting debtors through professional communication. We prioritize voluntary repayment and structured payment plans. For debtors, the process starts with a notice, followed by opportunities to settle or negotiate before any legal action is considered.

Debtor Rights and Obligations

Debtors have the right to fair treatment, accurate debt information, and privacy. They cannot be harassed or misled. At the same time, debtors are obligated to repay valid debts and maintain open, honest communication when arranging payment or settlement plans.

Guidance on Disputes, Payments, and Settlements

Disputes are reviewed promptly with supporting documentation. Payments can be made directly or through structured repayment schedules. Settlements may be negotiated where clients accept partial repayment as final resolution. All processes are handled transparently and in compliance with regulations.
